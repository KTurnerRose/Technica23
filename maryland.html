Maryland's Invasive Plants
Tree of Heaven
Scientific Name
Ailanthus altissima (ay-LAN-thəss al-TIH-sim-ə)
Common Name
Tree of Heaven
Native To
Northeast and central China, and Taiwan
Date of U.S. Introduction
First observed in MD in 1784
Means of Introduction
Ornamental
Impact
Crowds out native species; damages pavement and building foundations in urban areas
Japanese Barberry
Scientific Name
Berberis thunbergii
Common Name
Japanese Barberry
Native To
Japan
Date of U.S. Introduction
First observed in 1875
Means of Introduction

Introduced as an ornamental and promoted as a replacement for common barberry (Berberis vulgaris), which is a host for black stem rust

Impact

Forms dense stands that compete with native trees and herbaceous plants
